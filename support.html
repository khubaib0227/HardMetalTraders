Warranty Information

Our Warranty Promise

HMT Tools stands behind its products with a comprehensive lifetime warranty on defects in materials and workmanship.

  • Coverage Period: Lifetime
  • What's Covered: Manufacturing defects, material flaws, workmanship issues
  • What's Not Covered: Normal wear and tear, accidents, misuse, unauthorized repairs
  • Claim Process: Contact support with proof of purchase and serial number
